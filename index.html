About

Hey everyone! My name is Aditya Meenakshisundaram (Adi for short) and I am currently a student in the Texas A&M College of Engineering pursuing a degree in chemical engineering with a minor in business. I am also a part of the Craig and Galen Brown honors program here as well. I am passionate about bringing sustainable energy into the forefront of our energy landscape focusing on innovative methods to reduce our reliance on nonrenewable energy and promote environmental responsibility. I am interested in pursuing a career in energy consulting, where I can help organizations navigate the transition to cleaner and more efficient energy solutions.

Beyond school I love to dance, being a part of a dance team on campus and am involved in many different societies including TAMU AICHe (American Institute of Chemical Engineers) and more! In my free time I love to watch football, especially the Houston Texans, and travel all around the world! (follow @clicksbyadi on instagram for my lens of the world)
